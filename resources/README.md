### Core Textbooks
- **Nielsen & Chuang**: *Quantum Computation and Quantum Information* (2010)
  - The definitive reference for quantum computing
  - Comprehensive coverage of theory and applications
  
- **Mermin**: *Quantum Computer Science: An Introduction* (2007)
  - Accessible introduction for computer scientists
  - Minimal physics background required

- **Kaye, Laflamme & Mosca**: *An Introduction to Quantum Computing* (2007)
  - Mathematical foundations
  - Clear algorithm explanations

- **Watrous**: *The Theory of Quantum Information*
  - Advanced mathematical treatment
  - Quantum information theory focus
